About Me

Hi, my name is Claudette Cabahug, currently living in the beautiful little capital of New Zealand, Wellington.

Back home in the Philippines, I finished a degree in law and have had extensive experience in the insurance industry, particularly in the areas of sales, recruitment, and people management. At some point, I also dabbled in content writing for the web, ensuring client sites are search engine optimised, and it was something I really enjoyed.

Now, I am embarking on the journey of my life. I have joined Enspiral Dev Academy hoping to become web developer before I turn 40! I am keen to work on projects that will help parents like me make their life easier. However, I do welcome out of this universe, blow my mind away ideas that will help make the world a better place.
